Lena’s Artistic Beauty College can help you become a licensed cosmetologist. Our full service beauty school offers a complete cosmetology program that teaches all the required skills you need in order to apply for a beauty license. We have been helping people make their cosmetology dreams come true for over 50 years! Classroom education and hands-on salon hours are offered.

All types of hair care are taught and students learn all the basic techniques as well as popular and trendy styles. Aesthetics and nail care are also covered, including manicures and pedicures. In order to receive your license, completion of our 1,500-hour course is required.

Lena’s Artistic Beauty College was founded in 1960, by Lena Bryant, to help educate eager students on the skills and techniques available in the field of hair care and aesthetics. Today, Lena’s daughter, Denise Velasquez, continues this tradition using both the tried and true techniques she learned from her mother, as well as incorporating all the new and trendy styles and techniques of today’s fast paced world.



Our small, individualized classes and high success rate set us apart from typical beauty schools. We are a second-generation family-owned-and-operated cosmetology school and our lead teacher is backed by 18 years of experience teaching our classes.



We proudly serve the Chicago, Illinois area with a complete range of beauty school courses. We have built our reputation through hard work and determination, allowing us to become the leading beauty school in the area. We take great pride in providing you with top level service and promise complete satisfaction when choosing us for all of your beauty training needs.


Our company has developed highly customer service oriented practices because we believe that focusing on this will achieve the best results for our clients; we want them to succeed through us. Our professional instructors are highly trained and committed to providing the highest levels of quality available, to ensure you get the best quality of education.


Our skilled professionals strive to offer our students the latest in our field. We are always learning more and educating ourselves on the latest technologies and practices. This ensures that anyone who signs up for Lena’s Artistic Beauty College will be 100% satisfied.

Lena’s Artistic Beauty College offers you a unique chance to become a certified cosmetology instructor. Our 1,000-hour program offers all the courses needed to meet the state guidelines for becoming a cosmetology instructor. Teacher supervision is always in place for assistance and we guide you through the program an answer any questions you may have. We also offer a 500-hour program for those with at least 2 years of salon experience.


[ 75 hrs. ] Theory – Classroom Instruction

Limited to Orientation, Career Information, State Laws and Regulations, Professional Images, First Aid, Chemistry, Electricity, Job Seeking, and Professional Ethics.

[ 75 hrs. ] Bacteriology Decontamination, and Infection Control

Health, Public Sanitation, Methods, chemical Agents, Types, Classifications, Bacterial Growth, Biology, Infections, Infection Control, Products, Tools, Equipment Use and Safety


[ 75 hrs. ] Shampoos, Rinses, Scalp Treatments

Principles and Techniques of Treatments and Disorders of the Hair and Scalp, and Related Chemistry


[ 150 hrs. ] Hairstyling

Principles and Techniques of Wet Styling, Blow Drying and Waving, Finger Waving, Hairdressing


[ 200 hrs. ] Haircutting

Principles and Techniques of Sectioning, Removing Length or Bilk with Razor, Scissors, Clippers, Shears


[ 125 hrs. ] Hair Coloring, Bleaching

Principles and Techniques of Temporary, Semi – Permanent, and Permanent Colors, Bleaching, Tinting, Toning, Frosting, Special Effects, Problems


[ 200 hrs. ] Chemical Rearranging (Texture Services)

Principles and Techniques of Sectioning, Wrapping, Processing, Curling, Relaxing


[ 175 hrs. ] Manicuring, Pedicuring, Nail Extensions

Principles and Techniques, Massage, Manicuring, Pedicuring, and Advanced Nail Techniques


[175 hrs. ] Facials

Principles and Techniques of Skin Chemical Procedures, Massage, Facial Treatments, Cosmetic Application, Artificial Eyelashes, Removal of Unwanted Hair, Lash / Brow Tinting, Light Therapy


[ 50 hrs. ] Salon Business, Retail Sales
[ 50 hrs. ] Salon Business, Retail Sales

[ 200 hrs. ] Miscellaneous

To be applied by the instructor to strengthen student performance, supervised field trips or other related training.


[ 1500 hrs ] Total Hours

The above hour requirements must be met by each student in each category in order for the earned hours to be accepted by the state licensing board for examination. The generous portion of miscellaneous hours is to be applied as needed in curriculum – related areas or if the student desires to specialize in a specific area.


Continuing Education Classes

At Lena’s Artistic Beauty College, we offer continuing education for stylists and teachers to meet the requirements by the state for license renewal. Stylists are required to complete a 14-hour course and teachers must complete a 24-hour course for renewal.


Refresh Your Skills

We offer a refresher course which takes 250 hours. This is for people who have been out of the field for a while and would like to refresh their skills.


Flexible Schedules

School programs are offered on a full time or a part time basis. Full time is 10am to 6pm Tuesday to Saturday Part time is anything less than that, (with a minimum of 20 hours a week). The minimum time is from 10am to 2:00p or up.
