Lena’s Artistic Beauty College offers you a unique chance to become a certified cosmetology instructor. Our 1,000-hour program offers all the courses needed to meet the state guidelines for becoming a cosmetology instructor. Teacher supervision is always in place for assistance and we guide you through the program an answer any questions you may have. We also offer a 500-hour program for those with at least 2 years of salon experience.

[ 1500 hrs ] Total Hours

The above hour requirements must be met by each student in each category in order for the earned hours to be accepted by the state licensing board for examination. The generous portion of miscellaneous hours is to be applied as needed in curriculum – related areas or if the student desires to specialize in a specific area.

Continuing Education Classes

At Lena’s Artistic Beauty College, we offer continuing education for stylists and teachers to meet the requirements by the state for license renewal. Stylists are required to complete a 14-hour course and teachers must complete a 24-hour course for renewal.

Refresh Your Skills

We offer a refresher course which takes 250 hours. This is for people who have been out of the field for a while and would like to refresh their skills.

Flexible Schedules

School programs are offered on a full time or a part time basis. Full time is 10am to 6pm Tuesday to Saturday Part time is anything less than that, (with a minimum of 20 hours a week). The minimum time is from 10am to 2:00p or up.
